The Cheetah

The cheetah is a large cat native to Africa and central Iran.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Speed: 80 – 130 km/h (Running, Estimated)
  • Conservation status: Vulnerable (Population decreasing)
  • Mass: 21 – 72 kg (Adult)
  • Trophic level: Carnivorous

The cheetah is the fastest land animal, capable of running at 80 to 128 km/h, and as such has several adaptations for speed, including a light build, long thin legs and a long tail
